Welcome To My Webpage

This is My Final Project called "Obstacle Run." I worked on this with my partner, Jasmine Soares, and we decided to
create a game that everyone can play at all ages. You use the SPACE bar to jump and the DOWN arrow key to "duck"
or to quickly fall back down to the ground after your jump. Try to avoid the objects approaching you to earn points.
You have three lives, including the last, to get the highest score possible. GOOD LUCK!
